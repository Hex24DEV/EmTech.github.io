üí° What is an RFID Door Lock System?

RFID stands for Radio Frequency Identification. It uses radio waves to read data stored on tags (like key cards or fobs) without physical contact. In our system:

  • The RFID tag/card acts like a key.
  • The RFID reader scans the tag.
  • The Arduino microcontroller decides whether to unlock the door based on the tag's unique ID.
