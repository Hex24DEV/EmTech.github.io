INTRAMS WEEK - BLOG

By: Sagsagat, Mathew Andrei R.

12 STEM 13B

DAY 1: The Thrilling Kickoff

The start of Intramurals Week was bursting with excitement! The day began with a grand parade, where we proudly waved our banners and flags, representing our section and team with enthusiasm. The opening ceremony was a spectacle, introducing us to the much-anticipated Mr. and Ms. Intramurals and the talented players set to compete. As the games commenced, my friends and I eagerly watched the basketball match. Though our team didn't win, the experience left us energized and eager for the days ahead!

DAY 2: Close Matches and Team Spirit

My day started in a rush! I was assigned to play for my strand's badminton singles, but I didn't know the schedule yet. When I got the schedule, it was 8:30 AM, and I thought it was 10 AM because that was the time mentioned by our adviser. So I rushed to school and barely caught my queue time, but I managed to catch it and played the match. It was a good game, but luck wasn't on my side, so I lost and got 3rd place.

DAY 3: The Faculty Showdown & Championship Thrill

This was one of the most anticipated days: the Faculty Exhibition Game! Ever since Grade 11, I had looked forward to seeing our teachers step onto the court, not as educators but as spirited players. It was refreshing to watch them enjoy, laugh, and forget about their workload for a while. But the real thrill? It was championship day! With the final matches and the grand coronation of Mr. and Ms. Intramurals, the intensity was at an all-time high. Even though nerves ran wild, we all remembered that the heart of the event was about having fun. And that, we definitely did!

DAY 4: See You Next Year, Intrams!

And just like that, an unforgettable week came to an end. With memories filled with cheers, incredible performances, and moments of pure joy, we bid farewell to Intrams Week with one thought in mind: "UNTIL NEXT YEAR!" Can't wait to do it all over again!
