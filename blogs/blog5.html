A Magical Adventure: Our Field Trip

ZenZen Kyoto Park – A Touch of Japan in Cavite

Stepping into ZenZen Kyoto Park felt like teleporting straight to Japan! The park was filled with bamboo forests, koi ponds, and stunning torii gates that made every corner Instagram-worthy. Walking through the serene gardens, I could almost hear the whispers of the wind as we admired the cherry blossom replicas and traditional Japanese architecture. It was a peaceful start to our adventure, making us feel like we were in Kyoto itself!

Queens Strawberry Farm – Sweet, Juicy, and Fresh! 🍓

After our Zen moment, we headed to Queens Strawberry Farm, where the scent of fresh strawberries filled the air. Rows of bright red berries stretched out before us, just waiting to be picked. Some of us (including me!) couldn’t resist tasting a few while harvesting. Spoiler alert: They were delicious! We also got to try their famous strawberry taho and ice cream—pure heaven in a cup!

Enchanted Kingdom – Where the Magic Begins! 🎢✨

Now, here comes the highlight of our trip—Enchanted Kingdom! The moment we entered, excitement took over. The first stop? The Space Shuttle roller coaster—not for the faint of heart! The feeling of being upside down in the air was a mix of terror and thrill. But the fun didn’t stop there! We enjoyed the Rio Grande Rapids, getting drenched but loving every second of it, and screamed our lungs out on Anchor’s Away. Of course, we also had our fair share of magical moments in the Grand Carousel and a breathtaking view from the Wheel of Fate.

Final Thoughts: A Trip to Remember

From the peaceful gardens of ZenZen Kyoto Park to the adrenaline rush of Enchanted Kingdom, this field trip had everything. It was a perfect mix of relaxation, adventure, and endless laughter with friends. If this trip taught me one thing, it’s that the best memories are made when you step out of your comfort zone and enjoy the moment!

Until the next adventure! 🚀🎢🍓
